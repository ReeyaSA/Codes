FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:



-Who can rent a car?


Minimum age for hiring a car is 21 years and your license needs to be at least 1 year old. If you are a resident of Pakistan, you need to have a valid driving license along with NIC. If you are on a tourist visa then you need to have a valid home country license OR an international driving license and valid passport with visa stamp.


-Are there any delivery and collection charges?


Collection and Delivery charges in case of Self Drive are PKR 500, covering both.


-What are the delivery & collection timings?


The car delivery and collection time is from 8:00 am to 11.30pm.


-How can I pay the rent?


We accept cash and bank transfers at the moment.


-Where can the car be delivered?


We deliver all over Karachi. Note: The delivery is subject to availability.


-What is your fuel policy?


Customers are responsible for paying for their own fuel consumption. Each car is provided with a level of fuel and customers have to return the vehicle with the same level of fuel as they were given. We also provide an option to the customers, where they can pay for the number of kilometers done by the car at the specified rates for each car category, starting from the point the car leaves the office / Car Owner’s / Driver’s location.


-What do you mean by Per day


By Per day, we mean 10 Hours
